"This practice combines sustainable architecture with product design, video and installation art. The mix of skills is dynamic and unexpected. Backed by substantial and well-respected research, the practice creates work with social integrity. It addresses urgent issues of sustainability at both the urban and the building scale. It does so with refreshing clarity, sensuality and playfulness."
