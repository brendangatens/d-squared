Work with video is a core part of the practice's approach and output. Projects range from stop frame animation pieces, commissioned for arts and commercial contexts, to more documentary work in educational research and for stimulating debate about urban development.

Video into architecture: We are interested in the potential for video to be used to inform a design approach which derives from non-formal stimuli. We use video in the early stages of a project to study and record aspects of a site, and when a project is complete to record the dynamic qualities which are best captured by moving images. d-squared has been producing films and videos and teaching practical video making techniques for use in architecture continuously over the last decade.
