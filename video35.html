In collaboration with staff and students at the University of East London

An HEFCE funded documentary on digital video, for the 'Atelier Principle in Teaching' research programme, University of East London, 1999.

 

Projects are initiated, explored and developed through direct experience. Common phenomena and situations are acted, lived, recorded and tested as they happen. The participant designs from the inside of the experience, using the video camera to find, express and develop their idea.
