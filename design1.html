N1 Reactive Light Installation

London, 2004
Delancey

 
         
 
 

 

 
 
 

In collaboration with:Rolf Gehlhaar

The installation is designed to operate in a quiet zone of a shopping centre. The space is in constant but sporadic use 24 hours a day, when bars, the club or the cinema open and close, fill and empty.

 

 

The installation is interactive and registers the presence of passers-by who trigger light and sound sequences depending on their numbers and behaviour. When there are a few people the installation will register a quiet presence.
