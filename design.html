Installation design: Installation commissions invite a combination of video and architectural techniques to create dynamic three dimensional events, including the use of interactive and acoustic elements.

Product design: Our designs were originally made for outdoor and indoor gardens in an urban context. More recently we have made bespoke furniture for individuals and are developing designs for new school furniture. Our products have retailed through The Conran Shop in the UK, manufactured by ourselves. The springseat is currently being manufactured by EMU in Italy and distributed and sold globally.
